FAQ for Browse4Pets

What types of pets do you sell?

We offer a variety of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, fish, and small animals like rabbits and hamsters.

Do you provide pet food and accessories?

Yes! We have a wide selection of pet food, toys, bedding, and other accessories for all types of pets.

Are your pets vaccinated?

Absolutely! All our pets receive necessary vaccinations and health check-ups before being available for adoption or purchase.

Can I adopt a pet from your shop?

Yes, we support pet adoption! We have rescue pets available for loving homes. Contact us for adoption details.

Do you offer delivery services for pet supplies?

Yes! We offer home delivery for pet food, accessories, and supplies. Contact us to place an order.

What is your return or exchange policy for pet products?

Unused and unopened pet products can be returned or exchanged within 14 days of purchase with a receipt.
